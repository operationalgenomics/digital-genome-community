## Purpose

This Code of Conduct defines the minimum standards
of behavior expected from all participants in this project.

Its goal is to ensure respectful, focused, and productive
collaboration over the long term.

---

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- communicate respectfully
- focus on technical and conceptual matters
- assume good faith in discussions
- accept constructive criticism
- respect differing perspectives

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- harassment or intimidation
- personal attacks
- discrimination or exclusion
- abuse of authority or influence
- disruptive behavior that undermines collaboration

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces,
including repositories, pull requests, issues,
discussions, and related communication channels.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing
this Code of Conduct.

Enforcement actions may include:

- warnings
- temporary restrictions
- removal from participation

Actions will be proportional and documented.

---

## Reporting

Concerns about behavior should be reported privately
to the maintainers.

Public escalation should be avoided whenever possible.

---

## Final Note

This project values clarity, respect, and long-term thinking.

Disagreement is expected.
Disrespect is not.
